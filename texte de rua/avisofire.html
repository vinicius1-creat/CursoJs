If you discover a fire in your home

  1. Leave your property immediately closing all doors behind you.
  2. Call the fire service.
  3. Greet the fire crew and give details of the fire location.

If you see or hear of a fire alsewhere:

Leave your property if smoke or heat affects it or instructed. If you are in a communal area leave the building by the nearest exit.

DO NOT

  • Wait to collect personal belongings
  • Take risks
  • Use the lift
  • Run

CALL THE FIRE BRIGADE - DIAL 999

When the operator answers give your telephone number and ask for fire. Give the address where the fire is.
